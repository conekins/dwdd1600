Cultural Escape: Discover Japan on a Budget

Immerse yourself in the rich culture and history of Japan with this 7-day budget-friendly package. Explore the bustling streets of Tokyo, the serene temples of Kyoto, and the vibrant city of Osaka. Optional add-ons include guided tours of historic sites like Fushimi Inari Shrine, a visit to a traditional tea ceremony, and a day trip to the stunning Mount Fuji.

Included in the package:

  • Round-trip flights
  • 6 nights in 3-star Hotels (2 nights in Tokyo, 2 nights in Kyoto, 2 nights in Osaka)

  • Optional discounts on additional experiences
